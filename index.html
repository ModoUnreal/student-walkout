Student Walkout Against Gun Violence

American students live in the only developed country where mass shootings happen regularly. Demand that your state and federal lawmakers adopt deeper background checks, stricter licensing requirements, and limited access to semiautomatic firearms. Only then will students finally be safe to learn.

What This Is

This is a protest against gun violence and against Congress' unwillingness to uphold stronger gun laws.

This is a plea for help from students who have known school shootings all their life and demand change.

And lastly this is a message to say that we don't want any more empty words and passivity towards mass murder. We deserve a safer environment for us to learn in.

How will we send this message?

We will have multiple student walkouts in the next couple of months.

What we want is for students to attend school and promptly walk out of school at the designated time, issued below, and peacefully protest outside of your schools.

Through this our message will be effectively given, and that's exactly what is needed right now.
